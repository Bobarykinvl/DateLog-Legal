DateLog - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

TL;DR: Your dating data stays 100% private on your device. We only collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. No personal information is ever shared or stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data You Create

  • Names and contact information of people you add
  • Photos you choose to save
  • Ratings and notes about your interactions
  • Spending records for your dates
  • Achievement progress within the app

ðŸ”’ Important: ALL this personal data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics:

  • How often you use the app
  • Which features you use most
  • App crashes and performance data
  • General usage patterns (no personal content)

This data is anonymized and helps us improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Personal Data

  • Display in the app: Show your entries and achievements
  • Local processing: Calculate statistics and achievements
  • No sharing: Never shared with anyone

Analytics Data

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand which features are most valuable
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Make data-driven product decisions

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All personal data is stored in your device's secure Core Data database
  • Data is encrypted using iOS security features
  • Only accessible by the DateLog app
  • Not backed up to iCloud or other cloud services

Analytics Storage

  • Anonymous analytics are stored by Google Firebase
  • No personally identifiable information is included
  • Data is aggregated and anonymized

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data with anyone.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics (Google): Anonymous usage analytics only
  • Apple App Store: Handles subscription payments securely

Neither service receives any of your personal dating information.

5. In-App Purchases

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple
  • We don't store or access your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed through Apple's systems
  • Purchase data is anonymized for analytics purposes

6. Data Deletion

Deleting Your Data

  • Individual entries: Delete directly in the app
  • All data: Use "Delete all persons from memory" in Settings
  • App removal: Uninstalling the app removes all data

Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data is automatically deleted by Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 2-14 months).

7. Children's Privacy

DateLog is rated 17+ and is not intended for users under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 17.

8. International Users

DateLog stores all personal data locally on your device, regardless of your location. Anonymous analytics may be processed in the United States through Firebase.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through:

  • App Store update notes
  • In-app notifications
  • Updated version on this page

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Remove any or all data at any time
  • Control: Choose what information to store
  • Export: No export feature currently, but data remains on your device
